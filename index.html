Privacy Policy - SolChatPlus

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

At SolChatPlus, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting it under the principles of data sovereignty and decentralization. This policy describes how we handle information within our Web3 architecture.

1. Information We Collect

Unlike conventional applications, we do not collect or store Personally Identifiable Information (PII):

  • No Personal Data: We do not request names, emails, phone numbers, or contact lists.
  • Wallet Address (Public Key): This is the only identifier used to validate identity on the Solana network and facilitate messaging.
  • Technical Metadata: Minimal data for message delivery, which is end-to-end encrypted.

2. Blockchain Transparency

By using SolChatPlus, you interact directly with the Solana network. Any transaction recorded on the blockchain is public by nature, and we cannot delete or modify data confirmed on the network.

3. Third-Party Services and Wallets

We integrate with providers like Phantom or Solflare. We never have access to your seed phrases, private keys, or passwords.

4. Children's Privacy (Minors)

SolChatPlus is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any type of information from minors. If you are under 13, please do not use this application.

5. Information Security

While we use advanced security protocols, the ultimate responsibility lies with the user. Losing access to your wallet means a total loss of access to your SolChatPlus account and history.
