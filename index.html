Child Safety Standards – SolChat Plus

This policy applies to SolChat Plus, published on Google Play.

SolChat Plus has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any content, behavior, or interaction that involves, promotes, or facilitates CSAE is strictly prohibited.

In-App Reporting

Users can report content, messages, or users directly within the app. All reports are reviewed and may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent bans.

CSAM Enforcement

We strictly prohibit child sexual abuse material (CSAM). Any detected content will be removed immediately and reported to appropriate authorities.

Moderation

We use automated systems and manual review to monitor and prevent harmful activity on the platform.

Legal Compliance

SolChat Plus complies with all applicable child safety laws and cooperates with law enforcement when required.
