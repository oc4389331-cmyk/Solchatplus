Child Safety Standards – SolChat Plus

This policy applies to SolChat Plus, developed and operated by José Oscar Ernesto Romero.

SolChat Plus is a platform that enables user-generated content and interactions. We enforce a strict zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any content, communication, or behavior that involves, promotes, or facilitates CSAE is strictly prohibited.

Protection of Minors and Age Requirements

Users must meet applicable age requirements to use the platform. We implement additional safeguards to protect minors and reduce the risk of harmful interactions.

In-App Reporting, Blocking, and User Controls

SolChat Plus provides in-app tools that allow users to report messages, profiles, and content that violate our policies. Users can also block other users to prevent unwanted interactions.

All reports are reviewed within a reasonable timeframe. Actions may include content removal, account suspension, or permanent account termination.

Moderation, Prevention, and Enforcement

We use a combination of automated detection systems and manual review processes to proactively prevent abuse and identify harmful or illegal activity.

Severe or repeated violations result in permanent bans and restriction of access to the platform.

Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

The creation, upload, storage, or distribution of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) is strictly prohibited. Any detected material will be removed immediately and may be reported to appropriate law enforcement authorities.

Legal Compliance and Cooperation

SolChat Plus complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations. We cooperate fully with law enforcement authorities and escalate critical safety cases when required.
